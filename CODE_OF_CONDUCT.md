# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors and maintainers are expected to foster a respectful, professional environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Use clear, constructive technical feedback.
- Assume good intent and focus on code and outcomes.
- Respect different experience levels and communication styles.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or demeaning comments.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to project maintainers privately. For security-related behavior, follow `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict access for violations.
